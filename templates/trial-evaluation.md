## 6. Final Recommendation

Choose one and explain why.

### Continue

The model is working as-is well enough to keep running.

### Adjust

The model is promising, but needs specific changes before continuing.

### Expand

The model worked well enough to extend to another team or larger scope.

### Stop

The model did not improve outcomes enough to justify continuing.
